Things to do before
your tattoo appointment

⭑.ᐟ as your tattoo appointment approaches, we kindly encourage you to take a few important steps to help ensure a smooth and safe experience on the day of your session.

  • Hydration: drink plenty of water in the days leading up to your appointment. hydrated skin takes ink better and promotes faster healing.
  • Rest: being well-rested helps you manage pain and stay calm.
  • Eat a meal: eat a substantial meal before your appointment.
  • Skin preparation: moisturize the area & avoid sunburns.
  • Clothing: wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing.
  • Avoid alcohol & caffeine: do not drink 24 hours before your appointment.
  • Blood-thinning meds: avoid aspirin, ibuprofen prior to appointment.

Things to do after
your tattoo appointment

⭑.ᐟ following your tattoo appointment, we kindly encourage you to adhere to these aftercare guidelines to ensure the best possible outcome for your new tattoo.

DO'S

  • Keep it clean: gently wash 2-3 times daily with lukewarm water and fragrance-free soap.
  • Moisturize: apply thin layer of fragrance-free moisturizer after cleaning.
  • Wear loose clothing: avoid friction over the tattoo.
  • Protect from sun: avoid direct sunlight; use SPF after healed.
  • Be patient: allow natural healing.
  • Follow artist instructions: they know your piece best.

DON'TS

  • Remove initial film too soon.
  • Scratch or pick at scabs.
  • Submerge in water (swimming / tubs) until fully healed.
  • Apply harsh / petroleum products.
  • Avoid over-moisturizing.
  • Don't wear tight clothing that rubs the tattoo.
  • If infection signs appear, consult a doctor immediately.
